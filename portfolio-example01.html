LittleExplorer

Welcome to LittleExplorer.
LittleExplorer accelerate your daily search by recording your searched words and visited web pages so that you can easily revisit your search results and web pages. You are not necessary to use full-scale browser for your daily small and frequent searches, like Game results of your favorite team, plan for next travel, Ego search, etc.. LittleExplorer eliminate the hassle like heavy loading, too many tabs, deep hierarchal bookmark, etc, and focus on finding survey results in the fastest way.



BENEFITS AND FEATURES

  • Record the searched words and visited website to reuse these words for next search with one tap or revisit your webpages easily.
  • You can create new ideas through associative search by combining the searched words
  • Frequently used words are automatically collected
  • You can manage the words/pages as Favorite, categorized by section, or arrange the order of rows as you like.
  • Auxiliary search functions like using quote, excluding the specific words, etc. can be used from the list.
  • You can seamlessly move back and forth between browser and the lists.
  • You can use useful controllers which minimize your finger movement on the screen.
  • You can also link to the browser you usually use.
